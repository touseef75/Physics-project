Statement of Second Law of Thermodynamics

Lord Kelvin Statement:


It is impossible to consturct a heat engine, operating continuously in a cycle, which takes heat from a heat source at higher temperature and performs on equivalent amount of work without rejecting any heat to a heat sink at low temperature.

Rudolf Clausius Statement:


It is impossible to design a device which works on a cycle and produce no other effect other than heat transfer from a cold body to a hot body.
